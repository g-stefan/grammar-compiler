Theorem/Grammar Transform

1. E = A & B | A; => E = A & B?;
2. E = A & B | A & C; => E =  A & ( B | C );
3. E = E & A | B; => E = B & A*;
4. ((A)+)? => A*
5. E = A | A & B; => E = A & B?;
